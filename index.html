About

Medina Detailing is a one-man operation built on a simple idea — your boat should look the way it did the day you bought it. Every job is done by hand, on your dock, with no shortcuts.

— Detailing

What's included.

  1. 01

    Exterior Wash & Wax

    Full deck and hull rinse, hand wash with marine soap, hand-applied carnauba wax, and a microfiber dry-down. Chrome and stainless brought back to mirror.

  2. 02

    Interior Detail

    Vacuum, scrub, and condition every surface in the cabin and cockpit — vinyl, upholstery, teak, glass, and gelcoat. The boat smells new when it's done.

  3. 03

    Hull & Waterline

    Targeted treatment for scum lines, oxidation, and stain build-up at and below the waterline. Restores the original finish without aggressive compounds.

  4. 04

    Polish & Ceramic Protection

    Machine polish to remove oxidation and light scratches, followed by a marine-grade ceramic coating for long-term gloss and UV protection.
