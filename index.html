About

Born and raised on the Eastside, with two years at a local boat shop and years of freelance interior detailing. I specialize in Malibu, MasterCraft, Nautique, Cobalt, Axis, Chaparral, Supra, Moomba, Centurion, and Tigé — but I'll work on any boat or jet ski.

I come to you, wherever your boat lives — marina, lift, or private dock. This season I'm keeping a small client list so every boat gets real attention.

What's included.

  1. 01

    Interior Vinyl Wipe-down

    Seats, dash, and trim wiped and conditioned to keep the vinyl from cracking under the sun.

  2. 02

    Window Cleaning

    Windshield, side panels, and cabin glass cleaned streak-free for clear visibility on the water.

  3. 03

    Compartment Vacuum

    Glovebox, side compartments, and under-seat storage cleared of sand, leaves, and whatever else summer leaves behind.

  4. 04

    Exterior Wipe-down

    Full topside wipe to clear dust, water spots, and lake debris between bigger jobs.

Every job is quoted to your boat — its condition and where it's kept (covered slip, lift, marina). Ask about recurring detailing.
